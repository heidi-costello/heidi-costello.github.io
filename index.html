05  About Me

I'm happiest surrounded by people who push me to be a better animator, leader, and professional every day, as we build something we're all proud of.

Across two decades I've grown from animating and rigging characters on handheld and console titles to leading cinematic teams and directing animated content end to end. Whether I'm hand-keying a subtle facial beat, cleaning mocap into a believable performance, or blocking a layout sequence or camera move for a teaser, my desire has always been to make the audience feel something.

With a growth mindset and an unwavering passion for infusing characters and stories with life and charm, I want to continue contributing to rich narrative worlds, bringing unforgettable characters to life that leave a lasting impression.
